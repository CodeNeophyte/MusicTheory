Scale

public enum Scale

Represents scale of Notes by the intervals between note sequences based on a key Note.

  • major: Major scale.
  • minor: Minor scale
  • harmonicMinor: Harmonic minor scale
  • dorian: Dorian scale
  • phrygian: Phrygian scale
  • lydian: Lydian scale
  • mixolydian: Mixolydian scale
  • locrian: Locrian scale
  • custom: Custom scale by given base key and intervals.
